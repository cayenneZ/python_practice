Reading Code

	going through and make note of the following:
	1 Functions and what they do.
	2 Where each variable is first given a value.
	3 Any varialbe with the same names in different parts of the program.
	4 Any if-statements without else clauses.
	5 Any while-loops that might not end.
	6 Finally, any parts of code that you can not understand for whatever reason.
